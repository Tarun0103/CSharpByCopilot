# 4. Fibonacci

## Problem
Generate Fibonacci numbers (each number is the sum of the two preceding ones).

### Approaches
- Recursive (simple but exponential time without memoization).
- Iterative (linear time and constant space).
- Memoized recursion (top-down DP).

---

## ✅ Interview Prep (Fibonacci)
- Compare recursive vs iterative implementations.
- Explain why naive recursion is slow and how memoization fixes it.
- Be able to generate the first N Fibonacci numbers and compute the Nth efficiently.
